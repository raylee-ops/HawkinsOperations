# HawkinsOps 365-Day SOC Learning System
## Master Roadmap

**Target Role:** SOC Analyst Level 1
**Target Location:** Huntsville, AL
**Job-Ready Deadline:** Day 180 (May 1, 2026)
**Total Duration:** 365 Days

---

## Overview

This roadmap transforms you from security fundamentals to job-ready SOC analyst in 180 days, with an additional 185 days for advanced skill development, portfolio hardening, and continuous learning.

**Key Principle:** Hands-on every single day. No passive learning.

---

## Four-Phase Structure

### Phase 1: Foundation (Days 1-90)
**Goal:** Build core SOC fundamentals

- **Months 1-3**
- Master Windows & Linux logging
- Understand network fundamentals
- Learn SIEM basics (Wazuh)
- Develop log analysis skills
- Create first portfolio projects

**Job Readiness:** 25%

---

### Phase 2: Intermediate (Days 91-180)
**Goal:** Achieve job-interview readiness

- **Months 4-6**
- Threat hunting techniques
- Detection engineering
- Incident triage and analysis
- MITRE ATT&CK framework mastery
- Mock incident investigations
- Portfolio showcase development

**Job Readiness:** 100% (Target: May 1, 2026)

---

### Phase 3: Advanced (Days 181-270)
**Goal:** Deepen expertise and specialization

- **Months 7-9**
- Advanced forensics
- Malware analysis basics
- Purple team exercises
- Complex threat scenarios
- Advanced automation
- Specialized certifications prep

**Job Readiness:** 150%+ (Competitive advantage)

---

### Phase 4: Job Prep & Mastery (Days 271-365)
**Goal:** Maintain readiness, perfect portfolio, interview mastery

- **Months 10-12**
- Interview preparation
- Portfolio refinement
- Advanced capstone projects
- Mentoring/teaching others
- Industry networking
- Staying current with threats

**Job Readiness:** Expert L1, ready for L2

## Weekly Time Commitment

- **Daily:** 2-4 hours (minimum 2 hours)
- **Weekends:** Flexible catch-up or deep-dive projects

**ADHD-Optimized:**
- Pomodoro technique (25min focus, 5min break)
- Variety of activities each day
- Clear, achievable daily goals
- Immediate hands-on feedback

## Daily Structure

Each day includes:

1. **Learning Objectives** (3-5 specific goals)
2. **Concepts** (bite-sized theory, max 5 bullets)
3. **Lab of the Day** (hands-on practice)
4. **Commands & Tools** (Windows, Linux, Wazuh)
5. **Documentation** (portfolio building)
6. **Reflection** (skill tracking)
